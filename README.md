#  SkillBridge

**SkillBridge** is a decentralized platform that connects skilled professionals (engineers, carpenters, doctors, teachers, developers, artisans, and more) with clients — powered by **blockchain escrow** to ensure **trust, transparency, and instant payments**.  

SkillBridge solves unemployment and underemployment challenges by bringing **skills on-chain**, enabling professionals to showcase verifiable skills, receive fair pay, and build a global reputation without relying on centralized intermediaries.  

---

## Why SkillBridge?

Millions of skilled professionals in **Nigeria, Africa, and beyond** face:  
- ❌ **Unemployment & underemployment** despite having marketable skills.  
- ❌ **High platform fees** (10–20% on Upwork/Fiverr).  
- ❌ **Delayed or unfair payments** due to lack of trust.  
- ❌ **Limited access to global freelance markets** (FX restrictions, high remittance costs).  
- ❌ **No verifiable system** to prove experience and ratings.  

**SkillBridge** tackles these issues by combining:  
- ✅ **Escrow smart contracts** (funds are secured until work is completed).  
- ✅ **Instant withdrawals** in local currency (via stablecoins).  
- ✅ **Low fees** (2–5%).  
- ✅ **On-chain skill reputation** (transparent, tamper-proof).  
- ✅ **Borderless access** to global opportunities.  

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

1. **Client posts a job** → includes budget & funds escrow.  
2. **Professional accepts** → delivers work.  
3. **Funds are released** → automatically or on approval.  
4. **Provider withdraws instantly** to wallet/local currency.
